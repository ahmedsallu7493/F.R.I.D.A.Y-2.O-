Sure, here is a basic leave application that you can use:

Subject: Leave Application

Dear [Manager's Name],

I am writing to inform you that I will be taking [number of days] off from work starting [start date] and returning on [end date].

I apologize for any inconvenience this may cause and will do my best to ensure that my responsibilities are covered during my absence. If necessary, I am happy to work with you to make arrangements for someone to cover my duties while I am away.

Thank you for your understanding.

Sincerely,
[Your Name]
